Server Rules

  • Swear Words: Allowed but limited; do not take it to an extreme level.
  • Simping: Strictly prohibited. Offenders will be punished or permanently banned if found.
  • Quarrels: Life-threatening quarrels are not allowed. Offenders will be kicked out for 7 days.
  • Being "Sus" or Freaky: Allowed, but not in extreme ways. Do not harm the experience for others.
  • Spamming: Strictly prohibited in any channel. Offenders will be timed out.
  • Respect: Treat all members with respect. No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
  • Privacy: Do not share anyone's personal information without consent.
  • Follow Discord TOS: All members must follow Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

Please follow these rules to keep Tenjiku a safe, fun, and welcoming community for everyone!
