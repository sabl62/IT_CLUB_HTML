Welcome to Tenjiku Roles

Explore the various roles within the Tenjiku community. Click on any role to learn more!

  • Tenjiku's Owner: The Owner of Tenjiku, or simply the head, having access to all commands/permissions.
  • Tenjiku's Co-Owner: Co-Owners are the ones helping the owner manage the group.
  • Tenjiku's Moderator: Moderators are the ones who help the owner and co-owners manage the group.
  • Tenjiku's Support Team: Helpers who assist members with questions and issues.
  • Tenjiku's Member: Members can participate in discussions and access general channels.
  • Tenjiku's Division Leaders:
    Division A: The Highest Level of Division leaders, who help in implementing new plans to the server and have acquired this position by being the winner in the Fashion-Show.
    Division B: Second Highest Level of Division leaders, who assist Division A in their tasks, acquired by the most-interactive member.
    Division C: Organizes and manages events within the community, while helping the other division leaders.
  • Tenjiku's Bots: The fellow managers of Tenjiku, enhancing the overall experience.
