Our Story:

Tenjiku was founded with the vision of creating a welcoming space for KMC students to connect and share their passion. Our community is built on the principles of inclusivity, creativity, and fun. Whether you're a seasoned player or new to the scene, there's a place for you here! Tenjiku is not just a platform; it's a journey where every member contributes to the vibrant tapestry of our community. From gaming tournaments to collaborative projects, we strive to create an environment where everyone can thrive and enjoy their time together. Tenjiku was originally founded by a group of passionate KMC students who wanted to create a space where everyone could come together, share their interests, and have fun. Over time, it has grown into a thriving community that welcomes all KMC students, at aorund Mid-2024.
