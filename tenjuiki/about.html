About Tenjiku

Tenjiku is a vibrant online community where KMC students come together to connect, collaborate, and create lasting memories. Our mission is to foster a sense of family, encourage engagement, and provide a safe, fun, and supportive environment for everyone.

  • Community Focused: We strive to make KMC more of a family than just a school.
  • Engagement: Participate in events, tournaments, and activities with exciting rewards.
  • Supportive Team: Our owners, co-owners, and moderators are available almost 24/7 to help you.
  • Inclusive: All students are welcome, and everyone is encouraged to join and contribute.
  • Fun & Safe: We maintain a friendly atmosphere with clear rules to ensure everyone enjoys their time.

Whether you're looking to make new friends, show your talents, or just have fun, Tenjiku is the place for you!
